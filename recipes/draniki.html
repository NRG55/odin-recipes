Draniki the Potato Pancakes

Draniki recipe

Draniki are considered to be a Belarusian dish. Draniki is a high-calorie dish because the pancakes are fried in a lot of oil. Traditionally the potato pancakes were served at breakfast garnished with deep fried pork fat or salo.

The main secret of delicious Draniki is to serve them hot. They have a very tender taste and aroma which disappears after they were reheated. The potato pancakes are traditionally served with sour cream, however there are many other sauces that go well with the dish.

We have chosen the easiest and fastest recipe of Draniki.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs potato
  • 2 onions
  • 3 Tbsp flour
  • 1 egg
  • Melted butter or vegetable oil
  • Sour cream, greens and salted salmon for serving
  • Salt, pepper

Directions:

  1. Chop the onion and fry until golden brown.
  2. Finely grate the potatoes.
  3. Add salt, flour, a beaten egg, fried onions and mix thoroughly.
  4. Fry Draniki on a high flame on both sides until golden brown.
  5. Place the pancakes on a paper towel to get rid of excess oil.
  6. Serve immediately with sour cream, chopped greens and salmon.
